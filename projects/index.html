Personal Projects

  Calling and Messaging Tiles - Lightweight Windows Phone 8.1 and Windows 10 Mobile applications for quick calling and texting contacts.
You are able to quickly call and message your favorite contacts by pinning tiles on the start screen on your Windows phone and tapping only once on one of the tiles. The tiles display contact info like name, phone number, phone line you want to use to call them on. Free and paid versions are both available, the latter with extra functionality. The texting app also integrates well with Skype Preview on Windows 10 Mobile.
Download links: Calling Tiles, Calling Tiles +, Messaging Tiles, Messaging Tiles +
Also check out their Facebook page: /CallingAndMessagingTiles.
  you.haiku - Windows Phone 8.1 and Windows 10 Mobile application for creating Haikus with images and texts and sharing them over social networks.
